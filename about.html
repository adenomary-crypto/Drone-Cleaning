About us

The safest window cleaning company in Edmonton never leaves the ground

CleanTech Window Services was founded on a simple observation: the most dangerous, most expensive part of commercial window cleaning is getting a human to the glass. So we stopped sending humans.

Our story

Built by people who managed buildings, not just drones

We’ve sat on your side of the table, reviewing swing-stage quotes that doubled after engineering, fielding tenant complaints about platforms outside boardroom windows, and explaining fall-protection incidents to ownership.

Drone cleaning removes the entire category of risk. Our Transport Canada–certified pilots fly purified-water systems across Edmonton’s office towers, medical buildings, hotels and condominiums, delivering building facade cleaning that’s faster, safer and easier to defend in a budget meeting.

Edmonton, AB, home base

What we stand on

Three non-negotiables

Safety before speed

Every flight has a certified pilot, a visual observer and a written plan. If conditions aren’t right, we don’t fly, and you don’t pay.

Paperwork before pitch

COIs, RPAS certificates, flight authorizations and safety records, assembled per property and sent before you have to ask.

Portfolios before projects

We’d rather clean ten of your buildings every season than one of them once. Pricing, scheduling and reporting are built for that.
