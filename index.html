FAQ

The questions every PM asks first

Something we didn't cover? Contact us today or call (780) 780-8672.

Are you insured, and who carries the liability?

We carry $5M commercial general liability plus aviation-specific drone coverage. Because no workers are at height, your building's exposure drops compared to rope access or swing stages. COIs naming your property are issued same-day on request.

Are drone operations over my building even legal?

Yes. Our pilots hold Transport Canada RPAS Advanced certificates, and every flight is planned within Canadian Aviation Regulations — including airspace authorization near downtown Edmonton and the river valley corridor. You receive the flight plan and approvals as part of your file.

What about Edmonton winters and weather?

Our cleaning season runs April through October, and we build weather buffers into every schedule. If wind or rain grounds a flight, your building automatically moves to the next clear window — you're never billed for a day we didn't fly.

How tall a building can you clean?

Our fleet comfortably services 4 to 25 storeys — the range where drone cleaning delivers the biggest cost advantage over rigging. Taller towers are assessed case by case with tethered systems and airspace review.

What runs off the building — any detergent concerns?

We clean with deionized, purified water. No soaps, no solvents, nothing that needs containment or storm-drain permits. Runoff is plain water, which matters for LEED buildings and medical properties in particular.

How does the cost really compare to a swing stage?

On a typical 12-storey Edmonton office building, drone cleaning comes in 30–40% below a swing-stage quote once rigging, engineering and mobilization are counted — and the clean finishes in a day instead of a week. We'll price both side by side in your quote so you can show the comparison to ownership.
